Welcome

I am a software developer/designer living in Nova Scotia’s Halifax Regional Municipality. I was born and raised in Puerto Rico, United States, where I lived for 27 years prior to moving to Nova Scotia.

I first got interested in software development during my late teens, when I became fascinated with video game development. I taught myself a little bit of C++, JavaScript, and later C#. I also learned to use graphic suites like PainShopPro, and later Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator. I’ve also done some 3D modeling work with 3DS Max and Maya.

After moving to Nova Scotia, I decided to complete my post-secondary education, and I am now a 2nd-year Information Technology Student at the NSCC Institute of Technology campus, in Halifax (Though it is likely I’ve graduated by now and I’ve been too lazy to update this section.)

I am passionate about software development because I can use it as a creative outlet. Some people paint, sculpt, or sing. I program.

I am a problem solver. I enjoy finding creative solutions to challenging problems and use my skills to overcome them. I have an insatiable thirst to learn about new technologies and applying them in future endeavors.
